What is a Trade License?
An official permit issued by a UAE authority that legally allows a person or company to conduct specific business activities. It can be issued in the mainland or in a freezone.
Learn more →
Trade License validity
Your trade license must have at least 30 days remaining validity at the time of submission.
Business Activity
Commonly accepted activities include
  • General Trading
  • Sales
  • Manufacturing
  • Distribution / Wholesale
  • E-commerce
  • Retail
What is Tax Registration?
Your VAT registration documents issued by the Federal Tax Authority (FTA).
Learn more →
Which document do I need?
If your annual taxable supplies exceed AED 375,000 — you must provide a VAT registration document from the FTA. If your annual taxable supplies are below AED 375,000 — you can submit a non-VAT declaration instead.
Important
If your supplies grow to exceed AED 375,000 — either on or off the noon platform — you'll be required to provide a VAT registration at that time.
What is an authorized signatory?
The person legally authorized to sign documents and act on behalf of the business.
Learn more →
Whose identity documents should I provide?
The legal owner or manager of the business as named on your commercial registration.
